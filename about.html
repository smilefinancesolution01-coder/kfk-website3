About KFK

India's premium fresh fish marketplace, connecting coastal freshness to your kitchen with trust, quality, and speed.

Our Story

KFK was born from a simple belief — that every Indian family deserves access to the freshest, highest-quality seafood without compromise. Founded in 2024, we set out to revolutionize the seafood industry by building a direct supply chain from India's pristine coastal waters to your doorstep. Today, we're proud to be India's most trusted fresh fish marketplace, serving thousands of happy customers across the country.

Our Mission

To make premium, fresh seafood accessible to every Indian household by building a transparent, technology-driven supply chain that guarantees quality, freshness, and trust.

Our Vision

To become India's most loved and trusted seafood brand, setting the global standard for freshness, sustainability, and customer delight.
